My favorite 2000s RomComs

1. 13 going on 30 (2004)

*13 going on 30* is about how one wishes to be 30, but when they are, they wish to become 13 again.

  • Jenna Rink
  • Matt Flamhaff
  • Lucy Wyman

2. A Cinderella Story (2004)

*A Cinderella Story* is about 2 people texting each other anonymously, and falling inlove even without knowing how each other looks like..

  • Sam Montgomery
  • Rhonda
  • Austin Ames

3. Sydney White

*Sydney White* is about how the new girl isnt used to all the fancy and girly stuff in her moms sorority.

  • Tyler Prince
  • Rachel Witchburn
  • Sydney White
